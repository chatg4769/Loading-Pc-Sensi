PRO TIPS & STRATEGIES

Sensitivity Adjustment

Start with our recommended settings and adjust by 5-10 points at a time. Test in training mode after each change.

Movement Techniques

For rushers, use the "peek and fire" technique around corners. Keep your sensitivity slightly higher for quick reactions.

Recoil Control

Lower sensitivity (40-60) helps with recoil control for assault rifles. Practice dragging down while firing.

Sniper Aiming

Snipers should use lower sensitivity (25-45) for precise shots.

Device Optimization

Close background apps before playing. Use gaming mode if available. Reduce graphics for better FPS.
